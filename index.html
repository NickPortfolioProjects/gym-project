Welcome to Apex Performance Gym

Welcome to Apex Performance Gym, where every workout is a step toward becoming the strongest version of yourself. Whether you're here to build muscle, boost performance, or simply feel better in your everyday life, Apex gives you the tools, support, and atmosphere to make it happen. Founded by former rugby athlete Jordan “Jax” Carter, Apex was created to be more than a gym — it’s a community built on passion, resilience, and real results. Jax’s mission was simple: create a space where people of all backgrounds can train with confidence and push past their limits. Inside Apex, you’ll find state‑of‑the‑art equipment, expert coaches, and a friendly, motivating environment that makes every session feel like progress. We’re here to guide you, challenge you, and celebrate your wins — big or small. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or taking your first step into fitness, Apex Performance Gym is your home for strength, support, and transformation. Step in, show up, and let’s reach your apex together.
